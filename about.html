How to use

For years, the Mountain View APCS class has had the same daily log system: a student opens a document, copies and pastes its contents into their own document, and copies and pastes code into that document. It was tedious and reduced the amount of time a student had to code. We set out to change that. With a product in mind, we created the Daily Log Creator, which eliminates the need for copying, pasting, and opening slow-to-load tabs.
